Computer Science, Kayaking, and Space

Aloha! My name is Mitch. From a very young age I've been pretty much obsessed with computers. How do they work? What else can they do? Questions like these drove me down a never-ending path of self-discovery which has led to countless awards, certificates, and an insurmountable level of experience working with a large range of technologies spanning from embedded IoT firmware to distributed cloud infrastructure.

I am currently enrolled full time at the University of Utah, working towards my master's degree in Computer Science while maintaining a 3.91/4.0 GPA. In the summer I travel to the pacific northwest to spend my days interning with top tech companies like Facebook, my evenings kayaking on the lake, and my weekends in the mountains.

My ultimate dream in life is to join the ranks of NASA astronauts who have ventured to the moon, and do my part towards ensuring the future of the human civilization on celestial bodies outside of our Earth. This is a challenging dream which makes a master's degree look easy in comparison. In preparation for this, I am constantly furthering my knowledge and abilities in water survival, physics, piloting, and hands-on skills like welding and mechanics.

This website is a showcase of my hard work and determination that has gotten me to where I am today. Take a look around and feel free to shoot me a message. Mahalo for stopping by!
