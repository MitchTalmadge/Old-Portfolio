High School

In high school I competed with students from schools all across the state for the title of Sterling Scholar. Out of hundreds of very talented students, I had the honor of taking first place in the Computer Technology category.

In my first year of high school, I competed in the Central Utah Science & Engineering Fair (CUSEF) by submitting a project that my long-time friend and I had worked on since the beginning of middle school. We created MinecraftK12, an educational platform that turned Minecraft into a teaching tool. Our project won first place and awards from Intel and BYU.
